Baja Animal Sanctuary

Over a three week period this team was tasked to redesign this nonprofits online presents. Taking a human centered approach, we hoped to create a design that would emotionally engage the user.

Scope: UX/UI Design courseDuration: 3 WeeksTools: FIgmaRole: User Research UI/UX Design.

Team:Lauren Callie Bryan Christopher Laynette

Research

The team started this project with the creation Dan.

Dan started the conversation of the sites current user. Starting the conversation of the sites current pain-points and how the team could start to consruct a soiid foundation on the new iteration.

Competitor Analysis

Through out competitor analysis we found competiors told the story of our their foundation through the UI of the site. Colorful backgrounds coupled with imagery of adoptable pets. This all to gain the attention of the user and truly make them feel as one with the organization. This is somthing BAS lacked.

User Research

Through the creation of Dan and the competior analysis, the team dove into user research. Our user research provided many insights on what drives our user to websites and alloted insight to a better design.

The Problem

How can we create a design in a digital space that evokes an intrapersonal connection. .

  • Wireframing
    As the team begain wireframing. We kept in mind the insights from our potential users and what we learned from our competior analysis. The goal was to grab the users attention before the fold.
  • Testing
    Once we created a fully built out low-fi verison of the new BAS. We went back to our users for more insight.
